Coding and Computer Science

My experience with coding and computer science has been largely self-taught or circumstantial. I recently began the online Harvard CS50 course to establish a solid academic background in coding and computer science to better understand how it all works.

Below are some of my most recently-completed projects and problem sets.

Bulbs.c

For this project, I was tasked with turning a user-input message into the binary-equivalent of the message. This binary message would be represented by "light bulbs" being turned 'on' or 'off' given the 8-bit sequence.

This project helped me familiarize myself with C through data types, the "main" structure and command line arguments, and iterating through strings or integers.
